This Policy applies to all users worldwide and specifically addresses the requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

4. Data We Collect

4.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage and is not transmitted to our servers unless you participate in cloud-synced features (see §4.2):

  • Learning progress, lesson completion status
  • XP (experience points), level, streak count
  • Achievements, flashcard collection
  • App settings (theme, language preference, font size)
  • Daily tasks progress

4.2 Data Synced to Cloud (Firebase / Firestore)

The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication (Google LLC). No email address, phone number, or registration is required. Firebase assigns an anonymous user ID to your device session. The following data may be stored in our Firestore cloud database:

PUBLIC DATA — visible to other users of the App: Your chosen nickname (username), XP score, streak count, league tier, and avatar selection are displayed in the global leaderboard and club/league groups. By choosing a nickname, you consent to this data being publicly visible within the App.

  • Leaderboard entry: Nickname, XP, streak, league, avatar — publicly visible
  • League & club data: Group membership, weekly points, club boosts participation — visible to club members
  • Anonymous user ID: Firebase-assigned token (not linked to any personal identity)
  • Cloud sync metadata: Last sync timestamp, app version

4.3 Microphone / Speech Recognition

The App requests access to your device microphone to power speech recognition exercises (via expo-speech-recognition). Microphone access is used only during active speech exercises. Audio is processed on-device or via the platform's built-in speech recognition API; we do not record, store, or transmit raw audio files to our servers.

You may deny microphone permission at any time in your device settings. Speech exercises will be unavailable without it.

4.4 AI-Generated Audio (OpenAI Text-to-Speech)

AI-Generated Content Disclosure: Some audio content in the App (pronunciation examples, example sentences) is generated using OpenAI's Text-to-Speech (TTS) API. This audio is artificially generated by AI and is not recorded from a human voice. We send text strings (English phrases, words) to OpenAI's API solely to generate audio. No personal user data is sent to OpenAI for this purpose. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this processing: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

4.5 Subscription & Purchase Data (RevenueCat)

Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat, Inc. RevenueCat receives your device's app store receipt, an anonymous app user ID, and subscription status to verify and manage your premium access. RevenueCat does not receive your payment card information. All billing is processed directly by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). See revenuecat.com/privacy.

4.6 Analytics & Crash Reporting

We may use Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) and Expo's error reporting to collect aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (e.g., which screens are visited, crash logs). This data does not identify you personally and is used solely to improve the App.

4.7 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any registration credentials
  • Precise geolocation
  • Payment card or banking information
  • Contacts, photos, or any data outside the App
  • Raw audio recordings
